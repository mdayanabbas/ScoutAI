# ScoutAI Roadmap

## Near-term
- Backend persistence for follow-ups and cold DM drafts.
- User profiles and authentication.
- Richer source quality analytics.
- Better resume parsing and structured skill extraction.
- Saved search profiles.
- More complete demo fixtures and import/export tooling.

## Mid-term
- Email and calendar integrations with explicit user control.
- Smarter company intelligence.
- Founder/contact research surfaces.
- Interview prep workspace.
- Deployment-ready auth and RBAC.
- More robust application material versioning.

## Long-term
- Multi-profile job search.
- Team or cohort mode.
- Automated but review-gated application workflows.
- Cross-run learning for source quality and recommendation tuning.
