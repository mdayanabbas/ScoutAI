# Demo Checklist

## Pre-demo
- Backend is running.
- Frontend is running.
- PostgreSQL is running.
- Alembic migrations are applied.
- At least one resume is uploaded and active.
- At least one discovery run has completed or partially completed.
- At least three recommended jobs are visible.
- At least one job is saved or interested.
- At least one company is watched.
- At least one cold DM draft is saved or copied.
- At least one follow-up is tracked.
- Analytics page has non-empty data.

## During Demo
- Show Command Center first.
- Point out Demo Readiness.
- Run or preview Daily Scout.
- Open Review Queue.
- Open Action Center.
- Export a Markdown Application Pack.
- Show Cold DM Draft Builder.
- Show Follow-up Tracker.
- Show Pipeline.
- Show Company Watchlist.
- Show Analytics.

## Fallbacks
- Live source is slow: show run history, source diagnostics, and existing recommendations.
- HN returns noisy jobs: explain conservative matching and source-quality warnings.
- No top recommendations: open Recommended Jobs or Pipeline.
- No active resume: open Resume page and explain generic ranking still works.
- External APIs fail: show partial-data warning and Source Quality Advisor.
